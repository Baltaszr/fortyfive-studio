Santana for life

Santana, a Rum that delivers high quality and a fun social experience, aims to become a leading rum brand in Indonesia that is globally recognized. With the spirit of adventure and fun experiences, Santana invites consumers to enjoy every sip as part of their life journey.

Join the flavor adventure with Santana. Inspired by the legend of Djong Java, every sip is a tribute to the unique flavors of Indonesia. Created by Indonesians, Santana captures the essence of fun exploration.
