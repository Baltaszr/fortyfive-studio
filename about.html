About us

Story

In the summer of 2018, a handful of designers, strategists, and storytellers met in a tiny Jakarta studio, fueled by kopi tubruk and an unwavering belief: creativity could spark meaningful change. We drew inspiration from the year 1945—Indonesia’s moment of collective courage—and asked ourselves, “What if we channeled that fighting spirit into the local creative industry?” From those early conversations, Forty Five Studio was born: an independent, multidisciplinary creative atelier dedicated to defying expectations, amplifying underheard voices, and forging solutions that are as bold as they are sustainable.

Our Soul

Forty Five

At Forty Five Studio, soul is more than a buzzword—it’s our guiding star. Every project begins with heart: we invest time listening to your story, your dreams, and your challenges. We then merge deep cultural insight with data-driven strategy to co-create a brand ecosystem that resonates emotionally and performs strategically.  Impact is measured not just by awards or media impressions, but by real changes in brand perception, customer loyalty, and social value. We’ve helped local artisans find global audiences, nonprofits galvanize communities, and established corporations reinvent themselves for a new generation.

The Culture

Impact

1. Fearless Curiosity We foster a “what if?” mindset: experimenting with AR installations one week, refining brand archetypes the next. No idea is too audacious—only opportunities to learn.

‍2. Collaborative Energy From day one, we’ve been a living studio of cross-pollination: developers swap code snippets with copywriters; illustrators jam with strategists. Every voice matters, every perspective enriches the final work.

‍3. Purpose-Driven Action Sustainability isn’t an afterthought. Whether it’s sourcing eco-friendly printing or designing campaigns that champion social causes, we strive to leave a net-positive footprint on people and planet.

‍4. Respect & Play We work hard—and play hard. Weekly game nights, sketch-and-sip sessions, and monthly “share your quirky hobby” meetups keep our team energized, human, and creatively charged.

Positioning in Indonesia’s Creative Industry

Industry

Forty Five Studio stands at the nexus of tradition and innovation—bridging Indonesia’s rich cultural heritage with the demands of a globalized market. As an independent multidisciplinary studio, we occupy a unique space between large network agencies and boutique specialists. Our distinct positioning is defined by:
‍
‍• Spirit of 1945: Embodying Indonesia’s founding ethos of courage and unity, we tackle complex brand challenges with patriotic pride and fearless ingenuity.
‍
‍• Full-Spectrum Expertise: Unlike niche shops that focus on a single discipline, we deliver end-to-end solutions—from brand strategy and visual identity to digital experiences and performance marketing—under one collaborative roof.
‍
‍• Local Roots, Global Standards: Deeply rooted in Indonesian culture and community, we apply world-class methodologies and frameworks to ensure our work meets international benchmarks.
‍
• Sustainable Innovation: We are pioneers in eco-conscious creative practices, integrating circular design principles and social impact considerations into every project.
